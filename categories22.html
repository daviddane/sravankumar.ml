  • It is an open discussion giving a glimpse of assembly in our country. A topic is given which can be debated on basis of pros and cons such that the discussion heats up and gives out a new conclusion for a new era. Each session goes on for one hour and is based on how soon the discussion comes to a conclusion.

    No. of Rounds:1

    No. of persons per team:1

  • A photo fantasy turns out to be such activity, a participant will be provided with a witty picture and he/she should write a concept of ad depicting the picture.

    In second round, the best selected ad concept should be enacted or expressed in a wordy manner.

  • Participant needs to answer the question but with a trick played on his/her memory. The contestant needs to give the answer of previous question to the current question. It has multiple rounds with increasing speed of asking the questions, it purely depends on spontaneity of the student.

  • It is the competition for students based on their logic levels and also general knowledge based. We will test the skills regarding memory, timing and also selection of the right choice in given time.

    It has rounds judging the students and in each round the difficulty is geared up.

    No. of Rounds: 2

    No. of persons per team: 1 or 2

  • In this event participants are shown some picture matrix form that they have to identify the word behind or common in all pictures.

    There will be 2 rounds….

    Round 1: some pics with common word in them within the given time limit. Participant should cross the barrier score to get into round 2.

    Round 2: In this pics with songs or dialogues are shown they have to try to identify them. Max score in R2 will be given prize.

  • This is a fun event in which you can play your favourite video games like Counter strike, Blur, NFS, Flatout and many more with your friends.

    There will be exciting prizes for the winners.
