  • “SPEED, ULTIMATE GOAL OF OUR WORLD, BUT PROBLEM LIES IN CONTROLLING IT.”

    MAZE RUNNER:
    Now a-days everyone like racing but in this event we are considering speed as most prominent feature. Maze runner gives you an opportunity to test your skills and to win exciting cash prizes. In this competition you are required to build a rover that fulfils the specifications given.
    Max number of participants: 3/ team
    DESIGN SPECIFICATIONS:
    1) The device must be powered by rechargeable batteries. No other sources of energy are permitted.
    2) The device must be controlled either through a transmitter/receiver radio link or through an umbilical cord. An umbilical cord controller may not contain any batteries.
    3) The transmitter/receiver radio link may be any commercially available model controller.
    4) All radio controllers will be impounded and shut off at the competition, except during the team’s run
    5) Mechanical forces on the umbilical cord may not be used to help propel or control the vehicle. The umbilical cord must be detachable from the vehicle using a commercial connector. The umbilical card may not be a part of the supporting structure of the device. The umbilical cord may only be used to transmit the commands from the controller. Teams will be disqualified if, at any time, the umbilical cord is used to apply mechanical force to the device.
    6) Robot dimensions should not exceed 25*25 (L*B) (in cm) (with wheels).
    7) The battery voltage should not exceed more than 12 volts.
    RACING REGION:
    Racing region is the even terrain as the round number increases the path of the racing region is changed depending up on the number of teams.
    ROUND1:
    In this round the participant is given a racing path and he needs make his rover to pass through the track. Total time is considered for the bot to pass the track.
    Note: The length of the track may be changed by the judges depending upon the situation at the event.
    ROUND2:
    In this round the participant is given a maze to solve and he needs to find his way out of that maze and make his rover to pass through the track. Total time is considered for the bot to pass the track.
    Note: The path may be subjected to change depending on the situation and the decision of the judge is final.
    RULES AND REGULATIONS:
    1. Only team members can operate the bot.
    2. Team members are not permitted to touch either machines or those of their opponents once the game begins.
    3. Violation of any rules or specification leads to elimination.
    4. We are not responsible for damage of bots.
    BOT SPECIFICATIONS:
    1. SOFTWARE: EMBEDDED software’s ( AURDUINO ,RASPBERRY PI ,etc.)
    2. Robot dimensions should not exceed 25*25 (L*B) (in cm) (with wheels).
    3. Power supply should be less than or equal to 12 volts.
    4. Each team must have their own batteries. External power supply will not be provided.
    5. The power supply may be placed on the robot.
